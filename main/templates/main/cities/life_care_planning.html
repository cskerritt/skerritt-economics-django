Life Care Planning FAQs - {{ city.name }}

A life care plan is a comprehensive document that outlines the current and future medical, rehabilitation, and support needs of an individual with a catastrophic injury or chronic condition. It includes detailed cost projections for lifetime care needs in {{ city.name }}.

Most life care plans are completed within 4-6 weeks, depending on the complexity of the case, availability of medical records, and need for additional medical consultations or evaluations.

A defensible life care plan must be based on current medical literature, expert medical opinions, evidence-based practices, and current market pricing. It should be comprehensive, reasonable, and directly related to the individual's condition and functional limitations.
