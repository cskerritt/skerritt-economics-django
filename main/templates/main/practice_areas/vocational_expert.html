Expert Vocational Assessment Services

As a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor (CRC), Chris Skerritt provides expert vocational assessment and earning capacity evaluation services for attorneys nationwide. Our comprehensive vocational analyses help quantify the economic impact of injuries and disabilities on an individual's ability to work and earn income.

Core Vocational Services

  • Earning Capacity Evaluations: Pre-injury vs. post-injury earning potential analysis
  • Transferable Skills Analysis: Assessment of skills applicable to alternative occupations
  • Labor Market Surveys: Research on job availability in relevant geographic areas
  • Employability Assessments: Evaluation of competitive employability post-injury
  • Job Analysis: Detailed examination of physical and cognitive job requirements
  • Vocational Rehabilitation Planning: Cost projections for retraining and job placement
  • Loss of Earning Capacity: Quantification of diminished earnings over work-life
  • Worklife Expectancy: Statistical analysis of probable work duration

Our Vocational Assessment Process

  1. Records Review: Comprehensive review of medical records, employment history, and educational background
  2. Clinical Interview: When appropriate, direct assessment of the individual's capabilities and limitations
  3. Vocational Testing: Administration of standardized vocational assessment instruments
  4. Transferable Skills Analysis: Identification of skills applicable to alternative occupations
  5. Labor Market Research: Analysis of job availability and wage data in relevant markets
  6. Earning Capacity Calculation: Quantification of pre-injury vs. post-injury earning potential
  7. Report Preparation: Comprehensive report detailing findings and opinions
  8. Expert Testimony: Deposition and trial testimony as needed
