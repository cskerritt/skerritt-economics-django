Valuation Methodologies

Income Approach

  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF): Present value of projected cash flows
  • Capitalized Earnings: Single-period capitalization method
  • Excess Earnings Method: For businesses with significant tangible assets

Market Approach

  • Guideline Public Company Method: Multiples from comparable public companies
  • Guideline Transaction Method: Multiples from comparable M&A transactions
  • Prior Transactions: Analysis of prior company stock sales

Asset Approach

  • Adjusted Net Asset Method: Fair market value of assets minus liabilities
  • Liquidation Value: Net proceeds from asset disposition
  • Cost to Create: Replacement cost for startup businesses

Our Valuation Process

  1. Engagement Planning: Define purpose, standard of value, and valuation date
  2. Information Gathering: Financial statements, tax returns, operational data
  3. Site Visit/Management Interview: Understanding operations and strategy
  4. Financial Analysis: Historical performance and normalization adjustments

  6. Valuation Analysis: Application of appropriate methodologies
