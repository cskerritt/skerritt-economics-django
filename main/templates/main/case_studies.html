Wrongful Death

Construction Site Fatality Economic Analysis

Challenge: A 42-year-old construction foreman was killed in a crane collapse, leaving behind a spouse and three minor children.

Analysis: Calculated lost earnings capacity of $3.2 million based on work-life expectancy, projected promotions, and union benefits. Quantified household services value at $450,000 and loss of employer-provided health insurance at $380,000.

Outcome: Settlement of $4.1 million based on comprehensive economic report that withstood opposing expert scrutiny.

Business Valuation

Restaurant Chain Breach of Contract Damages

Challenge: Multi-location restaurant franchise claimed $8 million in lost profits due to franchisor's breach of territorial exclusivity agreement.

Analysis: Performed lost profits analysis using "but-for" methodology, examining 5 years of financial data, market analysis, and comparable restaurant performance metrics.

Outcome: Jury awarded $5.7 million after trial testimony established causation and reasonable certainty of damages.

Note: Case details modified to protect client confidentiality

Personal Injury

Traumatic Brain Injury - Young Professional

Challenge: 28-year-old software engineer suffered TBI in auto accident, resulting in cognitive impairments affecting career trajectory.

Analysis: Collaborated with vocational expert to assess residual earning capacity. Calculated $2.8 million in lost earnings based on pre-injury career path versus post-injury limitations. Included analysis of lost stock options and reduced advancement opportunities.

Outcome: Case settled for undisclosed amount after deposition testimony demonstrated clear economic impact methodology.

Note: Case details modified to protect client confidentiality

Medical Malpractice

Birth Injury Life Care Planning

Challenge: Infant suffered severe birth injuries due to delayed C-section, resulting in cerebral palsy requiring lifetime care.

Analysis: Developed comprehensive life care plan with medical team input. Projected lifetime medical costs of $8.7 million including therapies, medications, equipment, and home modifications. Calculated present value using appropriate discount rates.

Outcome: Structured settlement providing lifetime care funding based on detailed economic projections.
