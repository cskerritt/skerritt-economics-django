A business valuation expert is a professional who provides fair market value analysis, business appraisals, and shareholder dispute valuations. They serve as expert witnesses in legal proceedings and provide detailed analysis for attorneys and insurance companies.

Business Valuation services are needed when legal cases require expert analysis and testimony regarding fair market value analysis, business appraisals, and shareholder dispute valuations. This includes personal injury, wrongful death, employment disputes, and commercial litigation.

Christopher Skerritt holds multiple advanced degrees (M.Ed, MBA) and professional certifications including CRC, CLCP, ABVE/F, CVE, FVE, and IPEC. He has over 20 years of experience providing expert witness services.
