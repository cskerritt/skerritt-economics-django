Professional Life Care Planning Services

Skerritt Economics & Consulting provides comprehensive life care planning services for attorneys, insurance companies, and healthcare professionals involved in catastrophic injury cases. Our detailed analysis of future medical costs, care needs, and assistive technology requirements helps ensure injured individuals receive appropriate compensation for their lifetime needs.

Core Services

  • Future Medical Cost Projections: Comprehensive analysis of lifetime medical expenses
  • Assistive Technology Needs: Equipment and device requirements assessment
  • Home Modification Requirements: Accessibility and safety improvements
  • Long-term Care Planning: Attendant care and nursing needs analysis
  • Rehabilitation Services: Physical, occupational, and speech therapy projections
  • Transportation Needs: Vehicle modifications and travel requirements
  • Medication Management: Prescription drug cost projections
  • Quality of Life Assessments: Recreational and social participation needs

Life Care Plan Components

Medical Care

  • Physician Services: Specialists, primary care, and emergency services
  • Nursing Care: Skilled nursing and attendant care requirements
  • Diagnostic Testing: Routine monitoring and specialized evaluations
  • Surgical Procedures: Planned and potential future surgeries
  • Hospitalization: Anticipated admissions and length of stay
  • Medications: Prescription drugs and medical supplies

Rehabilitation Services

  • Physical Therapy: Mobility and strength maintenance
  • Occupational Therapy: Activities of daily living training
  • Speech Therapy: Communication and swallowing disorders
  • Respiratory Therapy: Breathing support and management
  • Recreational Therapy: Quality of life and social participation

Equipment and Supplies

  • Durable Medical Equipment: Wheelchairs, beds, lifts
  • Assistive Technology: Communication devices, computers
  • Prosthetic Devices: Artificial limbs and replacement schedules
  • Orthotic Devices: Braces and supportive equipment
  • Medical Supplies: Disposable items and consumables

Environmental Modifications

  • Home Modifications: Accessibility improvements and safety features
  • Vehicle Modifications: Adaptive driving equipment and accessibility
  • Workplace Accommodations: Job-related modifications
