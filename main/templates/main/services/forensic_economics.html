Professional Economic Loss Analysis

Skerritt Economics & Consulting provides comprehensive forensic economic services to attorneys, insurance companies, and legal professionals.   • Economic Life Expectancy: Work-life expectancy and earning capacity analysis

  • Household Services Loss: Value of domestic and personal services
  • Wrongful Death Analysis: Comprehensive economic impact assessment

  • Commercial Damages: Lost profits, mitigation, and business value losses

Our Methodology
