A vocational expert is a professional who provides employability assessments, earning capacity evaluations, and vocational rehabilitation planning. They serve as expert witnesses in legal proceedings and provide detailed analysis for attorneys and insurance companies.

Vocational Expert services are needed when legal cases require expert analysis and testimony regarding employability assessments, earning capacity evaluations, and vocational rehabilitation planning. This includes personal injury, wrongful death, employment disputes, and commercial litigation.

Christopher Skerritt holds multiple advanced degrees (M.Ed, MBA) and professional certifications including CRC, CLCP, ABVE/F, CVE, FVE, and IPEC. He has over 20 years of experience providing expert witness services.
