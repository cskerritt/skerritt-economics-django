Expert Vocational Rehabilitation Services

Skerritt Economics & Consulting provides comprehensive vocational expert services to attorneys, insurance companies, and rehabilitation professionals.   • Vocational Rehabilitation Plans: Return-to-work strategies and recommendations
  • Job Placement Analysis: Realistic employment opportunities assessment

  • Expert Testimony: Court and deposition testimony support
